What is pop it exactly?

This is a child’s game, in which the process can be compared to the bursting of a bubble wrap. But

Rules of the game

Players take turns and click on the bumps. It is imperative to finish the row that you

started. You can press a maximum of 3 bubbles at the same time (they must be near,

you cannot press several pimples at once, if they are not close to each other) The task

is to make the opponent go last. Who went lar - lost!
